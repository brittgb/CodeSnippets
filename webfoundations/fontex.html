Information architecture involves content, technology, an audience, and a purpose.

The content may be advertising, scheduling information for a social organization, book reviews, online course material, or anything else you can think of.

The technology involved includes creating and marking up the content, delivering it across many platforms and displaying it on many different types of browsers.

Graphics and other media can be incorporated.

Indexing, embedded keyword tagging and other aids for searching and retrieving information can be critical.

The audience is everyone in the world, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
The purpose? To inform, to sell, to show off, to reach out... in short any and every objective that daily communication among humans has ever (or will ever) have!
