Opportunities

I work full time in the non-profit sector, and have experience working with a broad group including youth, the elderly, low-income and/or homeless persons, and individuals with disibilities. If your non-profit serves a population that you think would benefit from yoga I am available on a volunteer basis for individual or group classes. Limitations apply depending on my current volunteer schedule. I'm also available for workshops with your staff, board, or volunteers centering around yoga, meditation, care of the soul, or building holistic health into your programs and services. For more information or to share an idea, visit my contact page .
