Scheduling

The recommendation for lessons are to schedule a series all at once to make sure you get the most out of your time. However, single sessions are fine, and we can work with your budget to find a plan that will work for you.

Rates

Average rates for private sessions (1-3 people, total payment due)
1 hour $60.00
1.5 hours $80.00
4 - 60 min sessions $400
8 - 60 min session $360
*for packages, full payment must be made at the begining of the first session. *travel fees apply for sessions that take place outside of Britt's home.

More Information

  • Payments can be made in advance via Paypal, or otherwise are due at the beginning of our session using cash, check, or credit card.
  • Please call or text at least 24 hours in advance if you need to cancel a lesson.
    • If you need to cancel last minute? First time is on me, second time will require a $30 cancellation fee due at the time of next lesson.
